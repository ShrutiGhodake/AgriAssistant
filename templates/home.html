Community

AgriAssistant is committed to fostering strong relationships within the agricultural community. We actively engage with farmers, agricultural organizations, and stakeholders to understand their needs and challenges. Through regular workshops, outreach programs, and collaborative initiatives, we aim to support sustainable farming practices and contribute to the growth of the agricultural sector.

Disease prediction

Plant disease prediction using image processing is an innovative approach to diagnose and identify diseases in plants. It involves the use of computer vision techniques and machine learning algorithms to analyze images of plant leaves or other parts and detect signs of diseases or abnormalities.
